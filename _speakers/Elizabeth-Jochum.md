Elizabeth Jochum is an Associate Professor at Aalborg University and Director of Robotics and Automation at RA Capital. Her work uses the visual and performing arts to rethink how robots and assistive technologies are designed and used. She was named one of the top 50 Women in Robotics by Robohub in 2021.
